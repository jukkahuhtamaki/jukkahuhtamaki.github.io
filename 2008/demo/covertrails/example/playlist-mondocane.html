Mondo Cane :: Mondo Cane @ Salsomaggiore, 2007/05/26

Mondo Cane is an upcoming album by Mike Patton. It will be cover versions of 1950s and 1960s Italian pop music, performed by Mike Patton with an orchestra of about 65 people. The album was originally planned for release in summer, 2008, but has been pushed back to "early '09."
